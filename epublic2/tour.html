What is Dragdis?

It's a Chrome extension that let's you collect all kinds of content from the web. Dragdis just pops when you start dragging something and all you need to do is just drop it to a folder.

Where do things I collect go?

All inspirations collected are stored on dragdis.com app. Beautiful, simple to search through, easy to tag, add notes or collaborate on.

  • Collect content with ease

    Come across something you love? Simply drag&drop it to any folder of yours. Yep, that’s about it. One drag&drop and it’s a done deal.

  • Collaborate with your team

    You can share your folder with anyone on your team — they instantly get to see all items being added in real time. Supporting one another’s creativity can be simple as that.

  • Keep things private

    No one can see stuff you collect to your folder. But hey, if you want to share it it’s as easy as copy-pasting a link.

  • Access on any device

    You can access your content from literally anywhere. Simply head to dragdis.com, be it on your phone, tablet or your girlfriend’s laptop.

  • Rediscover in a second

    Dragdis search is smart and is getting smarter everyday. Search for a title of any page you’ve collected, a note or a tag you added or simply type in “youtube.com” to see everything from this specific site.

  • Structure things in your own way

    Use 2-level folder system to create your folders and subfolders. Say, your design folder and graphic/ interior/ industrial design subfolders.

  • View and review

    All the beautiful things you collect are presented in a clean white space. Kinda like making a presentation without actually making one.

  • Use it for Free!

    You can use all of this (and even more!) for free, and for forever. There are no limits to how many items you can add to your Dragdis.
